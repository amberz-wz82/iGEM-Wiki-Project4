Our team is made from students from every year, every college, and every major. From gene-splicing in wet-lab to pursuing business endeavors to organizing outreach events, bright passionate students make this team thrive.

We recruit in the fall. Below is the breakdown of what a year in on Cornell IGEM looks like:

Spring: brainstorm next project and new member training (2 credits, 6-8 hrs/week)
Summer: on-campus project work (20+ hrs/week), all new team members (except for business and wiki subteams) are expected to stay in Ithaca over the summer
Fall: finish project and compete at iGEM jamborees (3-4 credits, 12-16 hrs/week)

We are not currently recruiting, but please see the section below for an example of what one of our applications looks like. Stay tuned for Fall 2020 recruitment!

If you have any questions, feel free to send them to cornelligem@gmail.com.
